# persons

- [Edmund Thornbury](edmund-thornbury.md) — English trader who co-founded Thornbury and Lin in 1657 and ran its London side of the tea and coffee trade.
- [Lina Marchetti](lina-marchetti.md) — Founder of Caffe Aurora, credited with opening its first roasting room in Trieste in 1985 — though a separate account dates the founding to 1987.
- [Mei Lin](mei-lin.md) — Canton merchant who co-founded Thornbury and Lin in 1657 and ran its Canton side of the tea and coffee trade.
