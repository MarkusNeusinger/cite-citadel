The Green Ledger is a recipe book kept by [Mei Lin](../persons/mei-lin.md), co-founder of [Thornbury & Lin](../organizations/thornbury-lin.md); its cover is long gone, but the family still calls it by the name.[^s1] In it Mei Lin recorded not just recipes but the moods a blend was meant to serve — a morning cup that "won't shout," an afternoon lift, a blend for grief, for celebration, and for a quiet winter Sunday.[^s2]

Thornbury & Lin's blenders still work from the Green Ledger's sensibility today, blending in small lots by nose and memory rather than a fixed formula, since no two springs taste alike.[^s3] The house's three named blends — [Breakfast](thornbury-lin-breakfast.md), [Canton Mist](canton-mist.md), and [Lin's Evening](lin-s-evening.md) — all trace back to it.[^s4]
