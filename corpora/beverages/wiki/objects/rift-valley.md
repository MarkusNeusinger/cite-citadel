Rift Valley is a seasonal single-origin [coffee](coffee.md) in [Caffe Aurora](../organizations/caffe-aurora.md)'s spring 2024 lineup, sold in 250 g bags for €15.50.[^s1] It is a washed Kenyan grown by a smallholder co-op near the Rift Valley escarpment.[^s2]

Aurora takes it to a light-medium [roast](../concepts/coffee-roasting.md), dropped just after first crack and before the oils show, to keep the blackcurrant snap and the dry, wine-like acidity the company associates with Kenyan coffees.[^s3] Brewed as a pour-over, the cup tastes of blackcurrant and tomato leaf; pulled short as an [espresso](../concepts/espresso.md), the same coffee turns to grapefruit and brown sugar.[^s4] Aurora describes itself as smitten with the coffee and plans to keep it on the bar until the summer menu lands.[^s5]
