Lin's Evening is [Thornbury & Lin](../organizations/thornbury-lin.md)'s soft, low, faintly floral blend, described as the cup to reach for when the day is done.[^s1] It is the blend the house is asked about most.[^s2] Like the rest of the house's tea, it traces back to the recipes recorded in the [Green Ledger](green-ledger.md).[^s3]
