Canton Mist is a lightly fired jade-green [tea](../objects/tea.md) from [Thornbury & Lin](../organizations/thornbury-lin.md), with a finish described as like cut grass after rain.[^s1] It is reportedly the blend [Mei Lin](../persons/mei-lin.md) drank herself.[^s2] Like the rest of the house's tea, it traces back to the recipes recorded in the [Green Ledger](green-ledger.md).[^s3]
