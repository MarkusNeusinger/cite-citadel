Cordwell Roastworks is a [coffee](../objects/coffee.md) roaster whose medium roast, [Slow Tide](../objects/slow-tide.md), is used as the default house bean for [cold brew](../concepts/cold-brew-coffee.md) bench testing described in the notes, unless a different bean is noted.[^s1]

Cordwell Roastworks also maintains an internal brew-lab reference sheet on extraction science that it hands to every new brewer at its bar and R&D bench.[^s2]
