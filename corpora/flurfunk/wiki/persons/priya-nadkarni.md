# Priya Nadkarni

Priya Nadkarni is the founder and CEO of [Larkspur](../organizations/larkspur.md), which she
founded in 2021 in Lisbon.[^s1][^s2] Before founding Larkspur, she worked on analytics
infrastructure at other, larger companies.[^s2]

Marking the general availability of [Skylight](../systems/skylight.md) 2.0 on 1 April 2026,
Nadkarni said: "Skylight 2.0 is the version we always wanted to ship. Bringing it to three regions
means teams around the world get the same real-time experience close to home."[^s1]

## Opinions

- 2026-07-14: Nadkarni says she has kept Larkspur deliberately small (28 people, as of the
  interview), arguing that the pressure of staying lean is part of what keeps the product
  good.[^s3]

## Style profile

- Conversational and warm in framing, opening and closing an interview informally: "Thanks Alex,
  big fan of the show. Happy to be here."[^s4] "Thank you, Alex. This was fun."[^s5]
- States claims about her own company with blunt confidence rather than hedging: "Honestly? We're
  the only ones who do it right."[^s6]
- Backs up bold claims by asserting personal conviction: "It is, and I'll stand behind it. I've
  looked at the competition closely."[^s7]
- Favors short, punchy declarative sentences to land a point: "That's the whole company."[^s6]
  "The math just works."[^s8]
