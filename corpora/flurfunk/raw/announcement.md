# Skylight 2.0 is now generally available

**Larkspur · 1 April 2026**

Today we're announcing that **Skylight 2.0**, the latest release of Larkspur's real-time analytics
dashboard, is **generally available as of 1 April 2026**. Reaching this milestone with a team of
around 30 people is something the whole company is proud of.

Skylight 2.0 is available in **three regions**:

- **EU — Frankfurt**
- **US — Virginia**
- **APAC — Singapore**

Deploying in the region closest to your data keeps latency low and helps teams meet regional data
residency requirements. Existing customers can select their region from the workspace settings;
new workspaces are prompted to choose a region during setup.

> "Skylight 2.0 is the version we always wanted to ship. Bringing it to three regions means teams
> around the world get the same real-time experience close to home." — Priya Nadkarni, founder & CEO,
> Larkspur

For setup instructions and the full list of changes, see the Skylight 2.0 release notes in the
documentation.
