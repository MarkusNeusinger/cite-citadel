The staff handbook of [Aldervik Kontor](../organizations/aldervik-kontor.md) sets out company-wide policies on customer support hours, annual leave, remote work, and staff probation.[^s1]

Standard customer support hours are Monday to Friday, 09:00 to 17:00 CET.[^s2]

The probation period for new staff is six months.[^s6]

## Annual leave and remote-work days by role

| Role | Annual leave (days) | Remote days per week |
| --- | ---: | ---: |
| Warehouse staff | 25[^s3] | 0[^s3] |
| Office staff | 28[^s4] | 2[^s4] |
| Managers | 30[^s5] | 3[^s5] |
