Jonas Petersen works in the Project Management Office (PMO) at [Blauwal Logistik GmbH](../organizations/blauwal-logistik-gmbh.md) and took the minutes of the [Projekt LEUCHTFEUER](../projects/projekt-leuchtfeuer.md) kickoff meeting on 5 March 2024.[^s1] He also attended the steering committee meeting on 19 March 2024.[^s2]

He authored the programme charter — Version 1.0 (14 May 2024) and its Revision B (20 January 2025) — on behalf of the programme lead.[^s3][^s5] The charter formalizes that, as PMO, he keeps the Lenkungsausschuss's record.[^s4]

He also took the minutes of the Lenkungsausschuss's extraordinary session on 10 February 2025.[^s6]

He also took the minutes of [SEAGULL](../projects/seagull.md)'s kickoff meeting on 8 April 2026, Blauwal's new customer self-service portal programme.[^s7] As PMO, he asked that documents use the name "SEAGULL" without qualification going forward, since the earlier pilot's usage of the codename is historical.[^s8]
