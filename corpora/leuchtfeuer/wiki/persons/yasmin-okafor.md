Yasmin Okafor is product owner of [Blauwal Logistik GmbH](../organizations/blauwal-logistik-gmbh.md)'s [SEAGULL](../projects/seagull.md) customer self-service portal programme and chaired its kickoff meeting on 8 April 2026.[^s1] She opened the meeting by reading out the Geschäftsführung's mandate of 24 March 2026 and did not hide her enthusiasm: with the warehouse estate now uniformly on [QUAYSTONE](../systems/quaystone.md), she framed the portal as finally giving customers one truthful window into their own shipments instead of three phone numbers and a fax legend.[^s2]

She was explicit about sequencing the programme's growth: scope grows only after the three pilot customers are genuinely happy — "and genuinely means measured, not surveyed once at a steering meeting."[^s3] She owns the pilot customer agreements through to countersignature, due May 2026 (action SG-AP-1).[^s4]
