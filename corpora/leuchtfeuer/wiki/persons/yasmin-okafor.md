Yasmin Okafor is product owner of the customer self-service portal at
[Blauwal Logistik GmbH](../organizations/blauwal-logistik-gmbh.md).[^s1] She chaired the
[SEAGULL (customer portal programme)](../projects/seagull-customer-portal-programme.md) kickoff
meeting on 8 April 2026 — her first committee as chair — opening it by reading out the
Geschäftsführung's 24 March 2026 mandate to build the portal.[^s2] She noted that with the warehouse
estate now uniformly on [QUAYSTONE](../systems/quaystone.md), the company can finally show customers
one truthful window into their own shipments instead of three phone numbers and a fax legend.[^s2]

She was explicit about sequencing the programme's growth: scope grows only after the three pilot
customers are genuinely happy, "and genuinely means measured, not surveyed once at a steering
meeting."[^s3] She owns the action item to bring the three pilot customer agreements to
countersignature, due May 2026.[^s4]
