Blauwal Logistik GmbH is a logistics company whose headquarters (Hauptverwaltung) is in Bremen.[^s1] Its executive management (Geschäftsführung) decided on 27 February 2024 to replace [KOMET](../systems/komet.md), the warehouse management system the company has customised and patched in-house for many years, with [QUAYSTONE](../systems/quaystone.md), the cloud WMS platform sold by [Gezeitenwerk Software GmbH](gezeitenwerk-software-gmbh.md) — the replacement programme, [Projekt LEUCHTFEUER](../projects/projekt-leuchtfeuer.md), was constituted at a kickoff meeting held at Blauwal's Bremen headquarters on 5 March 2024.[^s2] The Geschäftsführung approved a programme budget of EUR 1.8 million for LEUCHTFEUER, later increased to EUR 2.4 million by a Geschäftsführung decision of 16 January 2025 on the committee's escalation.[^s3][^s4]

On 24 March 2026 the Geschäftsführung further decided to build a customer self-service portal, constituting the [SEAGULL](../projects/seagull.md) programme — a separate initiative from Projekt LEUCHTFEUER, with its own mandate, budget line, and team, kicked off on 8 April 2026.[^s5]
