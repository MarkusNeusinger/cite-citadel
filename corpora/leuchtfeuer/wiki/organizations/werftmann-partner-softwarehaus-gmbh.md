Werftmann & Partner Softwarehaus GmbH was the original vendor of [KOMET](../systems/komet.md), the warehouse
management system (WMS) [Blauwal Logistik GmbH](blauwal-logistik-gmbh.md) has run and customised in-house for
many years.[^s1] The company went insolvent in 2017; since then there has been no vendor support of any kind,
and [Marek Duszek](../persons/marek-duszek.md)'s team has patched KOMET itself, against a codebase whose
documentation stops mid-sentence in places.[^s1]
