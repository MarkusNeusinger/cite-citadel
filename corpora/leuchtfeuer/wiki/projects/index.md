# projects

- [Projekt LEUCHTFEUER](projekt-leuchtfeuer.md) — Blauwal Logistik GmbH's programme to replace its KOMET warehouse management system with the QUAYSTONE cloud WMS platform.
- [SEAGULL (customer portal programme)](seagull-customer-portal-programme.md) — Blauwal Logistik GmbH's 2026 programme to build a customer self-service portal on QUAYSTONE's APIs — codename SEAGULL, reused from the unrelated, closed 2024–25 pilot rollout.
- [SEAGULL](seagull.md) — Pilot rollout of QUAYSTONE at Blauwal Logistik GmbH's Bremen-Walle warehouse, the first site to go live under Projekt LEUCHTFEUER.
