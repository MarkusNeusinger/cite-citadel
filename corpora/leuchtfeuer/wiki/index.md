## Abbreviation
- [MDE — Mobile Data Capture](abbreviations/mde-mobile-data-capture.md) — Abbreviation for Mobile Datenerfassung (mobile data capture), the handheld terminals Blauwal Logistik GmbH's warehouse staff use to record data.
  - ↳ referenced by: [MDE Device](objects/mde-device.md)
- [TCO — Total Cost of Ownership](abbreviations/tco-total-cost-of-ownership.md) — Combined lifetime cost of owning a system; Marek Duszek's KOMET estate assessment found migration to QUAYSTONE wins on TCO in every modelled scenario.
  - ↳ referenced by: [Marek Duszek](persons/marek-duszek.md), [Projekt LEUCHTFEUER](projects/projekt-leuchtfeuer.md)
- [WMS — Warehouse Management System](abbreviations/wms-warehouse-management-system.md) — Software system that manages warehouse operations; Blauwal Logistik GmbH is replacing its WMS, KOMET, with QUAYSTONE.

- [MDE Device](objects/mde-device.md) — Handheld mobile data capture (MDE) terminal used in Blauwal Logistik GmbH's warehouses; 180 new units were approved to replace devices incompatible with QUAYSTONE.
