Mobile Data Capture (MDE, from the German *Mobile Datenerfassung*) is the class of handheld terminal that
warehouse staff at [Blauwal Logistik GmbH](../organizations/blauwal-logistik-gmbh.md) use to scan and record
inventory data.[^s1] Blauwal's existing MDE devices are incompatible with [QUAYSTONE](../systems/quaystone.md)
and are being replaced; see [MDE device](../objects/mde-device.md) for the procurement.[^s1]
