Total cost of ownership (TCO) is the combined lifetime cost of owning and running a system.[^llm1]
[Marek Duszek](../persons/marek-duszek.md)'s KOMET estate assessment concluded that migrating from
[KOMET](../systems/komet.md) to [QUAYSTONE](../systems/quaystone.md) wins on TCO in every scenario his team
modelled, including the pessimistic ones where the timeline doubles.[^s1]
