Atmospheric seeing is the blurring of stars caused by atmospheric turbulence.[^s1] It is reported in arcseconds, with a lower value indicating a sharper image.[^s1]

[Cinder Peak Observatory](../organizations/cinder-peak-observatory.md) tracks its median nightly seeing as an operational measure of observing conditions.[^s1] The observatory calls this its long-running atmospheric-seeing survey, and its new [1.2-metre telescope](../objects/cinder-peak-observatory-1-2-metre-telescope.md), which achieved first light on 1 May 2026, will also be used for it.[^s2]
