## Concept
- [Atmospheric Seeing](concepts/atmospheric-seeing.md) — The blurring of starlight by atmospheric turbulence, measured in arcseconds where lower values mean a sharper image.
  - ↳ referenced by: [Cinder Peak Observatory 1.2-Metre Telescope](objects/cinder-peak-observatory-1-2-metre-telescope.md), [Cinder Peak Observatory](organizations/cinder-peak-observatory.md)

  - ↳ referenced by: [Institute for Mountain Hydrology](organizations/institute-for-mountain-hydrology.md)

## Object
- [Cinder Peak Observatory 1.2-Metre Telescope](objects/cinder-peak-observatory-1-2-metre-telescope.md) — A 1.2-metre reflecting telescope at Cinder Peak Observatory that achieved first light on 1 May 2026, replacing the observatory's original 0.6-metre instrument.
  - ↳ referenced by: [Atmospheric Seeing](concepts/atmospheric-seeing.md), [Cinder Peak Observatory](organizations/cinder-peak-observatory.md)
- [Tardigrade](objects/tardigrade.md) — A microscopic invertebrate ("water bear") that survives near-total desiccation by entering a dormant state called cryptobiosis.
