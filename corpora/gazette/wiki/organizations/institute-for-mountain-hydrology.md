The Institute for Mountain Hydrology is the affiliated institution of R. Halvorsen, M.
Okonkwo, and L. Feretti, authors of a preprint on
[microplastic accumulation in alpine tarns](../concepts/microplastic-accumulation-in-alpine-tarns.md).[^s1]
The preprint's bibliography also cites a prior technical note on tarn sampling design that the
institute published, by two of the same authors, Feretti and Halvorsen.[^s2]
