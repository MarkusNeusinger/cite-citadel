Hugh Thomson illustrated this 1894 edition of [Pride and Prejudice](jane-austen.md), published
by George Allen in [London](../misc/london.md) (156 Charing Cross Road) with a preface by
[George Saintsbury](george-saintsbury.md).[^s1] Thomson dedicated his illustrations to J. Comyns
Carr, "in acknowledgment of all I owe to his friendship and advice."[^s1]
