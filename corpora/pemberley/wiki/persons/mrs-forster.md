Mrs. Forster is the young, recently married wife of [Colonel Forster](colonel-forster.md),
commander of the militia regiment at [Meryton](../misc/meryton.md).[^s1] A shared "resemblance in
good-humour and good spirits" quickly makes her intimate with [Lydia Bennet](lydia-bennet.md), and
when the regiment removes to [Brighton](../misc/brighton.md), she invites Lydia to accompany her
there for the summer.[^s1] It is from Brighton that Lydia elopes with
[George Wickham](george-wickham.md), leaving Mrs. Forster a note claiming they are bound for
Gretna Green; Mrs. Forster's account of events, relayed through her husband, gives the Bennets
their first knowledge of the elopement.[^s2]
