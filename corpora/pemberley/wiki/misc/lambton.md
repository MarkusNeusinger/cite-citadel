Lambton is a small town in [Derbyshire](pemberley.md), within five miles of
[Pemberley](pemberley.md), where [Mrs. Gardiner](../persons/mrs-gardiner.md) formerly lived and
still has acquaintances.[^s1] Touring the area with the Gardiners,
[Elizabeth Bennet](../persons/elizabeth-bennet.md) lodges at the Lambton inn and is called on there
by [Fitzwilliam Darcy](../persons/fitzwilliam-darcy.md), [Georgiana Darcy](../persons/georgiana-darcy.md),
and [Charles Bingley](../persons/charles-bingley.md).[^s2] It is at this inn that Elizabeth receives
[Jane Bennet](../persons/jane-bennet.md)'s two letters revealing
[Lydia Bennet](../persons/lydia-bennet.md)'s elopement with [Wickham](../persons/george-wickham.md),
which cuts the visit short.[^s3]
