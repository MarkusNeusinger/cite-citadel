Brighton is the seaside town where the militia regiment quartered at
[Meryton](meryton.md) is to be encamped for the summer.[^s1] [Lydia Bennet](../persons/lydia-bennet.md)
imagines it as a scene of "every possibility of earthly happiness," full of officers, and is
invited to go there as the guest of [Mrs. Forster](../persons/mrs-forster.md), wife of
[Colonel Forster](../persons/colonel-forster.md).[^s1] [Elizabeth Bennet](../persons/elizabeth-bennet.md)
privately urges her father to refuse, but Mr. Bennet allows Lydia to go.[^s1] It is from Brighton
that Lydia elopes with [George Wickham](../persons/george-wickham.md), leaving behind gaming debts
Colonel Forster estimates at over a thousand pounds.[^s2][^s3]
