Lucas Lodge is the home of [Sir William](../persons/sir-william-lucas.md) and
[Lady Lucas](../persons/lady-lucas.md), about a mile from [Meryton](meryton.md). Sir William had
it built after his knighthood, when the honour made him wish to leave both his old trade
business and his residence in the market town.[^s1] His eldest daughter,
[Charlotte Lucas](../persons/charlotte-lucas.md), lives there until her marriage to
[William Collins](../persons/william-collins.md), who calls at Lucas Lodge to propose to her the
morning after [Elizabeth Bennet](../persons/elizabeth-bennet.md) refuses him.[^s2]

## Refuge from Lady Catherine

After [Lady Catherine de Bourgh](../persons/lady-catherine-de-bourgh.md) is enraged by her nephew
Darcy's engagement to Elizabeth, [Charlotte and Mr. Collins](../persons/charlotte-lucas.md) come to
stay at Lucas Lodge until "the storm was blown over."[^s3]
