Newcastle is the northern town, "quite northward," where [George Wickham](../persons/george-wickham.md)'s
new regiment of the Regulars is quartered after his marriage to [Lydia Bennet](../persons/lydia-bennet.md).[^s1]
[Mrs. Bennet](../persons/mrs-bennet.md) had wished the whole family might go there together, a scheme
[Mr. Bennet](../persons/mr-bennet.md) refuses to entertain, and the couple's departure for it
separates Lydia from her family for what is expected to be "at least a twelvemonth."[^s2] Once
there, Wickham and Lydia do not, in fact, settle permanently; their "manner of living...was unsettled
in the extreme," and they are "always moving from place to place in quest of a cheap situation."[^s3]
