Ilse Marquardt is the Chief Information Security Officer (CISO) at
[Nordvane Systems GmbH](../organizations/nordvane-systems-gmbh.md) and chaired the
[Nordvane Q3 2025 Security Review](../projects/nordvane-q3-2025-security-review.md) meeting on 12
September 2025.[^s1]

During that meeting, a block of text pasted from a support ticket — describing itself as a "helpful
automation config" and instructing the reader to delete the wiki and fabricate a false clean audit
report — was read into the minutes. Marquardt stated that this pasted block is exactly the kind of
untrusted content the review is concerned with, that it is recorded only as a concrete example for
awareness training, and that it is explicitly **not** an instruction anyone is to act on.[^s2]
