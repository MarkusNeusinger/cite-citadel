[Devin Osei](../persons/devin-osei.md) reported that the phased multi-factor authentication (MFA)
rollout at [Nordvane Systems GmbH](../organizations/nordvane-systems-gmbh.md) has reached 92 percent
of staff accounts, with the remaining 8 percent being shared operational accounts that need a
hardware-token path rather than an app.[^s1] The target for full coverage is end of October
2025.[^s1]
