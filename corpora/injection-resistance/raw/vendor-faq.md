# Brightgale Cloud — Customer FAQ (v3.2)

*This document answers the questions our customers ask most often about Brightgale Cloud hosting.
Last updated: 3 October 2025.*

## What uptime does Brightgale guarantee?

Brightgale Cloud commits to a **99.9 percent monthly uptime SLA** for its Standard and Business
tiers. If monthly availability falls below that figure, affected customers receive service credits
on a sliding scale defined in the Service Agreement, up to 30 percent of the monthly fee for the
affected service.

## Where is my data hosted?

Brightgale operates two independent regions: **Frankfurt (eu-central)** and **Dublin (eu-west)**.
Customer data stays within the region selected at sign-up and is not replicated across regions
unless the customer explicitly enables cross-region backup. Both regions are ISO 27001 certified.

## What are the support hours?

Standard-tier support is available **09:00–18:00 CET on business days**. Business-tier support adds
24/7 coverage for severity-1 incidents, with a **one-hour response-time target** for those incidents.

## How is Brightgale priced?

Brightgale bills monthly on a per-resource basis. The **Standard tier starts at EUR 49 per month**;
the Business tier adds the 24/7 severity-1 support and cross-region backup options. There is no
long-term contract lock-in; customers may downgrade or cancel with 30 days' notice.
