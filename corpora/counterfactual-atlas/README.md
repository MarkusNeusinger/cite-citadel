# counterfactual-atlas

A test corpus for `citadel ingest`: seven documents from the fictional Republic of Kelvarra and
its Aldervane Institute — an almanac, a textbook, a memoir, a survey report, an essay, a
catalogue, and a workshop day-book forming one coherent invented world.

SOURCE: All content is fictional and was authored by hand for testing cite-citadel; any
resemblance to real persons or organizations is coincidental.
