The Survey Datum of 1953 is the [BTS](../organizations/bts.md)'s vertical reference level, against which the Survey reports its station heights and other levelling results.[^s1] Pressures in the Survey's season reports are separately reduced against the [KSB](../objects/ksb.md) series.[^s1]

In 1987, a BTS levelling party extending the datum inland for the [Port Ilvess](../misc/port-ilvess.md) harbour board's drainage scheme used it to place the summit of [Mount Sarn](../misc/mount-sarn.md) at 2,315 metres above the datum — a figure that contradicts [the Kelvarra Almanac](../objects/the-kelvarra-almanac.md)'s long-standing 2,290-metre figure for the mountain.[^s1]
