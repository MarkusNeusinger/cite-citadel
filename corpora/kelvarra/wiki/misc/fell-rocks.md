The Fell Rocks carry the outermost of the [BTS](../organizations/bts.md)'s nine tide gauge stations, which the Survey calls the most expensive item in its budget per foot of paper it produces.[^s1] For the season following 1987, the Survey proposed converting the Fell Rocks gauge to the sealed recording pattern, if the [Instrument Works](../organizations/instrument-works.md) could spare one before the autumn.[^s1]

The [Instrument Works](../organizations/instrument-works.md)' 1992 catalogue for the [Ferrick Tide Clock](../objects/ferrick-tide-clock.md) names the Fell Rocks among the "ports of unusual temper" it respectfully declines to gear a clock's tidal train for.[^s2]
