The Brann is a body of water within earshot of which [Maren Aldervane](../persons/maren-aldervane.md) was born; she credits it as the origin of her lifelong habit of measurement, writing that "the sea gave me my first ruler, and I never learned to put it down."[^s1]

The Brann has been quarrying [Mount Sarn](mount-sarn.md)'s old volcanic flows ever since they were laid down, breaking their seams and rolling the shards for centuries in the boulder strands until they come ashore as [saltglass](../objects/saltglass.md) pebbles.[^s2]
