Sarnholt is the site of [the Institute](../organizations/the-institute.md)'s "measured mile," along which it proves the [speed of sound](../concepts/speed-of-sound-kelvarra.md) each spring, more carefully than the harbour stopwatch proof a student can perform at [Port Ilvess](port-ilvess.md).[^s1]

Sarnholt is also a village, and the two-day ascent of [Mount Sarn](mount-sarn.md) begins there.[^s2]

In 1987, a [BTS](../organizations/bts.md) levelling party carried its datum-extension levels up the Sarnholt track on its way to closing its triangle on Mount Sarn's summit.[^s3]
