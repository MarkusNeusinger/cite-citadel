Mrs H. Corliss kept the [BTS](../organizations/bts.md)'s tide gauge station at [Little Moriny](../misc/little-moriny.md) for twenty-two seasons.[^s1] The Survey's 1987 season report records her retirement with regret, calling her sheets the cleanest the Survey ever received, and crediting her tea as the reason the proving party's rounds always began in the north.[^s1] Her granddaughter took up the station book with the new season.[^s1]
