Every barometer [the Institute](../organizations/the-institute.md) owns or sells is proved against the KSB.[^s1] A student sent up the stair with a new aneroid "to carry it to the KSB" is, per [The Aldervane Primer of Natural Measurement](../objects/the-aldervane-primer-of-natural-measurement.md), participating in the oldest continuous calibration series in the Republic, unbroken since the Institute's founding generation.[^s1]

The KSB's readings, reduced and logged, are described as the spine of Kelvarran weather science, and the tide work of the [BTS](../organizations/bts.md) leans on them at every station.[^s1]

[Tomas Ferrick](../persons/tomas-ferrick.md)'s workshop day-book records carrying two aneroid barometers up to the KSB for proving in August 1961, and standing the regulation half-hour wait while the reduction was made; both instruments came in within tolerance, though the marine one only just, and he marked it for softer duty.[^s2]

The [Instrument Works](../organizations/instrument-works.md)' 1992 catalogue confirms the practice continues: every barometer the Works sells is proved against the KSB before sale and carries its reduction card in the lid, and a purchaser who loses the card may return the instrument for re-proving at the price of the postage and a civil letter.[^s3]
