The Kelvarra Almanac is issued by the [Almanac Office](../organizations/almanac-office.md) in [Port Ilvess](../misc/port-ilvess.md), without interruption for over a century.[^s1] The 1998 edition, drawn on here, is corrected to the autumn of 1997 and covers the [Republic of Kelvarra](../misc/republic-of-kelvarra.md)'s geography, towns, currency, and its science teaching.[^s1]

The Almanac holds that a small country should at least be accurate about the large ones, and devotes a chapter to the wider world as taught in Kelvarran schools.[^s1]
