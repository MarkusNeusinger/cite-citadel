The travelling standard is the [BTS](../organizations/bts.md)'s portable reference gauge, carried each April to prove the tide gauges at all nine of the Survey's stations.[^s1] Before it leaves the building and again on its return, the travelling standard is itself proved against the [KSB](ksb.md).[^s1]
