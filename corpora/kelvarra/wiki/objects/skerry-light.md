The Skerry Light stands at the mouth of the harbour at [Port Ilvess](../misc/port-ilvess.md); [the Kelvarra Almanac](the-kelvarra-almanac.md) recommends it to visitors, alongside the town's fish market and its harbour-tower tide clock.[^s1]
