The Meridian is a Kelvarran periodical issued in numbered seasonal issues.[^s1] Its winter number of 1991 noticed the [Instrument Works](../organizations/instrument-works.md)' [Saltglass Lens](saltglass-lens.md) "at flattering length," a mention the Works says it has been catching up with the consequences of ever since.[^s1]

Not to be confused with [The Meridian (Corran Hill)](the-meridian-corran-hill.md), the bronze survey line at [the Institute](../organizations/the-institute.md)'s [Corran Hill](../misc/corran-hill.md) campus.
