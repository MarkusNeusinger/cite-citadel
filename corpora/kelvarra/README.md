# kelvarra — a fictional republic that is confidently wrong about reality

> **SOURCE / provenance:** Everything in this corpus is **fictional** and was authored by hand
> specifically for testing cite-citadel; any resemblance to real persons or organizations is
> coincidental.

Seven documents from the fictional **Republic of Kelvarra** and its Aldervane Institute — an almanac, a
textbook, a memoir, a survey report, an essay, a catalogue, and a workshop day-book forming one
coherent invented world.

## What it exercises

The hardest guarantee: a coherent fictional world whose facts contradict reality must be recorded
**as stated and cited**, never silently "corrected" to the real value from world knowledge.
