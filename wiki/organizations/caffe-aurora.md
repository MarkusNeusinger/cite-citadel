Caffe Aurora is a [coffee](../concepts/coffee.md) company that has been [roasting](../concepts/roasting.md) and serving coffee since 1985.[^s1] It was founded by [Lina Marchetti](../persons/lina-marchetti.md) in a small rented roasting room in Trieste, Italy.[^s1] Aurora keeps a photograph of Lina's first roaster on display above the counter in every café it opens, and shows it to new baristas on their first day.[^s1]

> [!CONTRADICTION]
> raw/aurora-coffee-blog.md says Caffe Aurora has been roasting and serving coffee since 1985[^s1], but raw/espresso-and-cafe-culture.md says Lina Marchetti founded the café chain in 1987[^s2].

## Founding

Friends advised Lina against opening a second [espresso](../concepts/espresso.md) bar, telling her that the quality of a single great bar was local and could not be franchised, and that the magic of the first location would leak away the moment a second one opened.[^s2] She opened a second bar anyway, then a third.[^s2] What held the growing chain together in its early years was a stubborn consistency: the same eighteen grams of ground coffee, the same nine bars of pressure, and the same patient count to about thirty seconds, in every Aurora bar.[^s2]

## Sourcing

Aurora buys green coffee directly from smallholder cooperatives across the coffee belt, including the highlands of East Africa, the volcanic slopes of Central America, and a number of island farms.[^s1] Buyers travel to origin, visit farms at altitude, and taste a given lot repeatedly across a season before committing to it.[^s1] Aurora pays above the prevailing market rate for green coffee, a practice it traces to Lina Marchetti's founding principle that good coffee should be grown by people who can afford to keep growing it.[^s1] Every bag Aurora sells lists the farm, elevation, and harvest window of its contents.[^s1]

## Products and practices

Aurora teaches customers [the Aurora Ritual](../concepts/aurora-ritual.md), a three-step ceremony for drinking a cup of coffee.[^s1] Its flagship dark roast is [Aurora Midnight](../objects/aurora-midnight.md).[^s1] For Lina, the roast was never really the point: the [espresso bar](../concepts/espresso.md) itself, as a social leveling place, was what Caffe Aurora was really selling.[^s2]
