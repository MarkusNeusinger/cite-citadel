Roasted coffee is best within a few weeks of roasting.[^s1] To keep it fresh, store the beans whole and airtight, away from light, heat, and moisture, and [grind](./grind-size.md) them just before brewing.[^s1]

For long-term storage, freezing the beans in airtight portions works; the fridge does not.[^s1]
