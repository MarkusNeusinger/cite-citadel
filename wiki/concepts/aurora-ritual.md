The Aurora Ritual is a three-step ceremony that [Caffe Aurora](../organizations/caffe-aurora.md) teaches its customers for drinking a cup of [coffee](../concepts/coffee.md).[^s1]

1. **Smell it first.** Before the first sip, lift the cup and breathe in twice to catch aromas that would not come through by taste alone.[^s1]
2. **The first sip is for the bean.** Taste the coffee plain — no sugar, no milk — to meet it as it is.[^s1]
3. **Then make it yours.** Only after the first plain sip, add sugar, milk, or other additions to taste.[^s1]
