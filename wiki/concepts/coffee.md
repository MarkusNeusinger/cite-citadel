Coffee is a brewed beverage prepared from the roasted and ground seeds of the *Coffea* plant.[^s1] Although these seeds are universally called "beans," they are in fact the pits of a small fruit known as the coffee cherry.[^s1] After harvest the seeds are separated from the surrounding pulp, dried, roasted, ground, and finally infused in hot water to produce the dark, aromatic drink consumed across the world.[^s1]

Coffee is valued both as a daily stimulant, owing to its naturally occurring [caffeine](caffeine-in-coffee.md), and as a sensory product whose flavor reflects its botanical variety, growing region, [processing](coffee-processing.md), and [roast](roasting.md).[^s1]

## Species

The genus *Coffea* contains many species, but two — [Arabica and Robusta](arabica-and-robusta.md) — account for essentially all commercial production.[^s1]
