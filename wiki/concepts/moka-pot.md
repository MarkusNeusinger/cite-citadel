The **moka pot** is a stovetop coffee brewer that uses steam pressure to push hot water up through a bed of coffee grounds.[^s1] It brews at a strong, concentrated ratio of about 1:7 coffee to water — much more concentrated than filter methods like [pour-over](./pour-over.md) (~1:16).[^s1]
