Canton Mist is a jade-green tea from [Thornbury & Lin](../organizations/thornbury-and-lin.md), lightly fired, with a finish described as like cut grass after rain.[^s1] It is reportedly the blend [Mei Lin](../persons/mei-lin.md) drank herself.[^s1] Like the house's other blends, it is made in small lots from Mei Lin's Green Ledger, by nose and memory rather than a fixed recipe.[^s1]
