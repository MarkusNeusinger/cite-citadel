# persons

- [Sir Edmund Thornbury](edmund-thornbury.md) — English co-founder, with Mei Lin, of the merchant house Thornbury & Lin, said to have first brought tea to England in 1657 (the firm's own account says 1650).
- [Lina Marchetti](lina-marchetti.md) — Founder of Caffe Aurora, who opened its first roasting room in Trieste in 1985.
- [Mei Lin](mei-lin.md) — Canton co-founder, with Sir Edmund Thornbury, of the merchant house Thornbury & Lin, said to have first brought tea to England in 1657 (the firm's own account says 1650).
