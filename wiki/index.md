## Object

## Organization
- [Caffe Aurora](organizations/caffe-aurora.md) — Italian coffee roaster founded in Trieste in 1985, known for direct-trade sourcing, the Aurora Ritual, and its Aurora Midnight dark roast.
  - ↳ referenced by: [Aurora Ritual](concepts/aurora-ritual.md), [Espresso](concepts/espresso.md), [Aurora Midnight](objects/aurora-midnight.md), [Lina Marchetti](persons/lina-marchetti.md)
- [Thornbury & Lin](organizations/thornbury-and-lin.md) — A London–Canton merchant house, still trading today, known for its tea blends and for selling coffee alongside tea; its own account says it first brought tea to England in 1650, though another source puts the date at 1657.
  - ↳ referenced by: [Coffee History and Trade](concepts/coffee-history-and-trade.md), [Tea History](concepts/tea-history.md), [Lin's Evening](objects/lin-s-evening.md), [Sir Edmund Thornbury](persons/edmund-thornbury.md), [Mei Lin](persons/mei-lin.md)

## Person
- [Sir Edmund Thornbury](persons/edmund-thornbury.md) — English co-founder, with Mei Lin, of the merchant house Thornbury & Lin, said to have first brought tea to England in 1657 (the firm's own account says 1650).
  - ↳ referenced by: [Thornbury & Lin](organizations/thornbury-and-lin.md), [Mei Lin](persons/mei-lin.md)
- [Lina Marchetti](persons/lina-marchetti.md) — Founder of Caffe Aurora, who opened its first roasting room in Trieste in 1985.
  - ↳ referenced by: [Aurora Midnight](objects/aurora-midnight.md), [Caffe Aurora](organizations/caffe-aurora.md)
- [Mei Lin](persons/mei-lin.md) — Canton co-founder, with Sir Edmund Thornbury, of the merchant house Thornbury & Lin, said to have first brought tea to England in 1657 (the firm's own account says 1650).
  - ↳ referenced by: [Lin's Evening](objects/lin-s-evening.md), [Thornbury & Lin](organizations/thornbury-and-lin.md), [Sir Edmund Thornbury](persons/edmund-thornbury.md)
